You know how rockstars tour to support their albums? Who is to say a book author could not do the same?

Through a couple of coincidences, it looks like I'll be touring Europe this April. I'll start the tour from Finland and visit countries like Norway, Germany, Czech, Slovakia, and Austria during it. I will be talking mostly about webpack during the tour unless there's demand for other topics.

Note that I have time especially during the weeks at Norway and Germany. This is your chance to bug me in person!

I've listed the tour dates below with registration where possible. The list will be updated as I have more information available.

23-24.3 - Helsinki, Finland#

  • 23.3 - React Helsinki - Re-imagining Webpack - 20 mins + QA, first go!
  • 24.3 - wwweeklies - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 45 mins + QA, from basic to advanced topics fast.

1-9.4 - Norway, Oslo#

  • 3.4 - ReactJS Norway - Re-imagining Webpack - 20 mins + QA, second go!

9-19.4 - Germany#

  • 11.4 - Berlin - NewStore - Webpack - From Apprentice to Journeyman - 45 mins + QA, Register at Eventbrite or see the Meetup page for more information.
  • 14.4 - Cologne - CologneJS - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 4h (with breaks!) + QA
  • 18.4 - Berlin - Reactive Roadshow - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 90 mins + QA

19-26.4 - Czech Republic and Slovakia#

  • 19.4 - Prague - Reactive Roadshow - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 90 mins + QA
  • 20.4 - Hradec Králové - Reactive Roadshow - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 90 mins + QA
  • 24.4 - Brno - Reactive Roadshow - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 90 mins + QA
  • 25.4 - Bratislava - Reactive Roadshow - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 90 mins + QA
  • 26.4 - Košice - Reactive Roadshow - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 90 mins + QA

27.4 - Vienna, Austria#

  • 27.4 - Vienna - Reactive Roadshow - Webpack - From Apprentice to Master - 90 mins + QA

Conclusion#

See you on the tour!
